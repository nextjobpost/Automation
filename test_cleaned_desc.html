Cabinet Secretariat Opens 25 Group A Senior Field Officer Posts Through GATE

Here is a golden chance for engineering and science graduates who have a valid GATE score and dream of a top central government job. The Cabinet Secretariat, Government of India has released Advertisement No. 01/2026 for the post of Senior Field Officer (Technical) — a Group A, Gazetted post in Level-10 of the Pay Matrix. A total of 25 posts are to be filled across five technical subjects, and the selection is based on your GATE score plus an interview, so there is no separate written exam to clear.

This is a direct recruitment for Indian nationals, and the monthly pay comes to around ₹1,30,000 (approximately) at New Delhi. The last date to submit your application is 12 July 2026. Because this is one of the most prestigious central government jobs available right now, competition will be strong but rewarding. In this guide, we explain the vacancies, eligibility, GATE requirement, and the offline application process in very simple words.

Eligibility Criteria — Cabinet Secretariat Senior Field Officer 2026

Please read this section very carefully, because both the degree and the GATE score are compulsory. Even a small mistake in eligibility can lead to rejection.

Educational Qualification

A candidate must possess a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Technology OR a Master’s degree in Science from a recognised University or Institution in the relevant subject. In addition, the candidate must have a valid GATE score of 50% or above (i.e., a GATE score of 500 or more) in the corresponding subject. A valid GATE score from GATE 2024, 2025 or 2026 is accepted. Candidates from a computer science and IT background have the most openings here, with 10 posts in the CS subject alone.

Age Limit

The candidate’s age should be not exceeding 35 years as on the closing date. Age relaxation is available for various categories (SC/ST/OBC, Ex-Servicemen, PwD, etc.) as per the standard orders of the Central Government. Make sure you calculate your age correctly as on the last date.

Salary and Pay — What a Senior Field Officer Earns

This is where the post becomes very attractive. The Senior Field Officer (Technical) is appointed in Level-10 of the Pay Matrix, which carries a basic pay band of ₹56,100 – ₹1,77,500. Including all allowances such as Dearness Allowance and House Rent Allowance, the monthly emoluments come to around ₹1,30,000 in case of posting at New Delhi.

Apart from the strong salary, you get the status of a Group A Gazetted Officer, government job security, pension benefits, and a respected role within the Cabinet Secretariat. For technically qualified candidates, this combination of pay and prestige is hard to find elsewhere.

Selection Process — How Candidates Will Be Chosen

The selection is simple to understand and rewards your GATE performance. Here is how it works:

  1. All valid applications are arranged in order of merit based on the GATE score, separately for each subject.
  2. Candidates equal to five times the number of vacancies in each subject are shortlisted and called for interview.
  3. Final selection is based on the GATE Score (maximum 100 marks) and the Interview (maximum 20 marks) together.

How to Apply for Senior Field Officer (Technical) 2026

This recruitment uses an offline (postal) application. There is no online form, so follow these steps carefully:

  1. Type the application on A-4 size paper in English capital (block) letters using black or blue ink only.
  2. Fill in every column — do not leave any column blank, and attach a recent passport-size photograph.
  3. Attach self-attested copies of your certificates (Matriculation onwards), the valid GATE Score Card, and category certificate if applicable.
  4. Place the complete application in an envelope and send it through ordinary post to the Post Bag address mentioned in the .
  5. Make sure your application reaches on or before 12 July 2026 — late applications will not be accepted.

Documents Required with the Application

Keep self-attested copies of these documents ready before you post your application:

  • Mark-sheets and certificates of Matriculation, 10+2 and your Bachelor’s/Master’s degree
  • Proof of age (Matriculation certificate, Birth certificate, PAN card or Passport)
  • Valid GATE Score Card for the corresponding subject
  • Caste/Category certificate in the prescribed Central Government format, if applicable
  • Recent passport-size photographs
